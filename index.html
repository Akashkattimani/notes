about ssit college

Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology is situated in Tumkur in Karnataka state of India. Established in 1979, SSIT is a Private UnAided college. The college is accredited by AICTE, NAAC. Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology offers 19 courses across 3 streams namely Engineering, IT, Vocational..Popular degrees offered at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology include B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA, MSc..Besides a robust teaching pedagogy, Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology is also a leader in research and innovation.Focus is given to activities beyond academics at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology, which is evident from its infrastructure, extracurricular activities and national & international collaborations. The placement at Sri Siddhartha Institute of Technology is varied, with recruitment options both in corporates and public sector as well as entrepreneurship.
